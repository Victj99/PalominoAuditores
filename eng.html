Audits

Through our Audit Division we offer the following specialized services to the private and public business sector, international organizations, NGOs, MYPES and union, educational and associative sectors:.

  • Audit of the financial statements.
  • Management and results audit.
  • Audit of projects to international organizations and NGOs.
  • Operational audit.
  • Audit of special exams.
  • Internal audit and organizational design outsourcing.
  • Audit of credit operations and loan portfolio.
  • Account audit of the financial statements.
  • Audit of previously agreed procedures.
  • Audit of income and expenses.
  • Audit of processes in the procurement of goods and services.
  • Audit for change of management and taking of charges.
  • Audit aimed at detecting irregularities and fraud.
  • Audit of statistical information prior to publication.
  • Specialized training for internal audit staff.
  • Review of compliance with the State Contracting Law.
  • Diagnosis of the Internal Control System in the COSO environment.
  • Money and asset laundering assessments.

Accounting and Financial Reports

Through our Accounting and Financial Affairs Division we offer the following specialized services:

  • Accounting, legal and tax advice and update.
  • Accounting analysis and financial evaluation.
  • Regularization of accounting accounts.
  • Review of assets, liabilities and accounting adjustments.
  • Review of the application of IAS / IFRS.
  • Financial evaluation and analysis and reports.
  • Formulation of accounting manuals of standards and procedures.
  • Accounting for companies in liquidation and Associations.
  • Analysis and adjustments of tax obligations.
  • Formulation of closing and extinction balances of companies in dissolution.
  • Review of accounting cost systems.
  • Accounting for special purposes.
  • Technical Studies of Transfer Pricing.

Business Consulting and Advice

Through our Consulting and Advisory Division we offer the following specialized services:

  • Design of policies, standards and procedures manuals.
  • Diagnosis of internal control systems - COSO.
  • Evaluation of economic and financial management.
  • Design of strategic plans and business plans.
  • Modeling and redesign of processes.
  • Review and formulation of actuarial studies.
  • Taking physical inventories of warehouses, fixed assets and other tangibles.
  • Analysis and special reviews of accounting operating processes.
  • Advice for ISO certification is Company valuation.
  • Valuation and Merger of companies.

Tax and legal advice

The topics to be developed in the different training and professional development programs are referred to:

  • Audit and internal control.
  • Taxation.
  • Management and cost accounting.
  • Information systems.
  • Data processing.
  • Personnel selection service.
  • Evaluation Service
